## Corpora

### General Corpora

- [DWDS Corpus](https://www.dwds.de/d/korpora) - Digital Dictionary of the German Language with extensive text corpora.
- [DEREKO](https://www.ids-mannheim.de/digspra/kl/projekte/korpora/) - German Reference Corpus by the Institute for the German Language.
- [DeReKo-2019-I](https://www.ids-mannheim.de/digspra/kl/projekte/korpora/releases/) - Release of the German Reference Corpus.
- [GerManC](https://www.llc.manchester.ac.uk/research/projects/germanc/) - German Manchester Corpus of historical German texts.
- [TIGER Corpus](https://www.ims.uni-stuttgart.de/forschung/ressourcen/korpora/tiger/) - Syntactically annotated corpus of German newspaper text.

### Specialized Corpora

- [TüBa-D/Z](https://uni-tuebingen.de/fakultaeten/philosophische-fakultaet/fachbereiche/neuphilologie/deutsches-seminar/abteilungen/computerlinguistik/ressourcen/corpora/tueba-dz/) - Tübingen Treebank of Written German.
- [C-WEP](https://www.cl.uzh.ch/de/texttechnologie/research/cwb/cwep.html) - Corpus of Written Educated Professional German.
- [SdeWaC](https://www.ims.uni-stuttgart.de/forschung/ressourcen/korpora/sdewac/) - Web corpus of German from the .de domain.
- [DIRNDL Corpus](https://www.ims.uni-stuttgart.de/forschung/ressourcen/korpora/dirndl/) - Discourse Information Radio News Database for Linguistics.

### Historical Corpora

- [DTA](https://www.deutschestextarchiv.de/) - German Text Archive (Deutsches Textarchiv).
- [GerManC-GS](https://www.llc.manchester.ac.uk/research/projects/germanc/) - Genre-balanced corpus of early modern German.
- [Referenzkorpus Altdeutsch](https://www.deutschdiachrondigital.de/) - Reference corpus of Old High German and Old Saxon.
- [Referenzkorpus Mittelhochdeutsch](https://www.linguistics.ruhr-uni-bochum.de/rem/) - Reference Corpus of Middle High German.

## Tools

### Corpus Analysis

- [CQPweb](https://cwb.sourceforge.io/cqpweb.php) - Web-based corpus analysis tool.
- [SketchEngine](https://www.sketchengine.eu/) - Corpus analysis and terminology extraction tool.
- [AntConc](https://www.laurenceanthony.net/software/antconc/) - Freeware concordance program for corpus linguistics.
- [NoSketchEngine](https://nlp.fi.muni.cz/trac/noske) - Open-source corpus search system.

### Language Learning

- [German-English Dictionary App](https://german-english-dictionary.devtheworld.jp/) - For scientifically-efficient modern learning.
- [Linguee](https://www.linguee.de/) - Dictionary and translation search engine with corpus examples.
- [PONS](https://de.pons.com/) - Online dictionary with example sentences.

### Text Processing

- [spaCy German Models](https://spacy.io/models/de) - Industrial-strength Natural Language Processing models.
- [SoMaJo](https://github.com/tsproisl/SoMaJo) - Tokenizer and sentence splitter for German texts.
- [Germalemma](https://github.com/WZBSocialScienceCenter/germalemma) - Lemmatization for German language text.
- [German NLP](https://github.com/adbar/German-NLP) - Curated list of open-access/open-source NLP resources for German.

## Academic Texts

- [OPUS](http://opus.nlpl.eu/) - Open parallel corpus with academic texts.
- [Project Gutenberg (German)](https://www.gutenberg.org/browse/languages/de) - Free ebooks in German.
- [Freiburg Corpus](https://www.freiburg-corpus.de/) - Academic corpus of spoken German.
- [ZAS Database of Clause-Embedding Predicates](https://www.zas.gwz-berlin.de/en/research/databases.html) - Linguistic database for German predicates.
